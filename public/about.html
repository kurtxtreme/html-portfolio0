About Me

Katlego Nkuna is an Operations Coordinator with a passion for technology, sports, and problem-solving. He works at the intersection of sports technology, digital operations, and project management, helping organizations use technology to deliver better experiences and more efficient workflows.

Currently at ItsHappening Africa, Katlego supports the delivery of digital sports events through platform management, live streaming operations, event administration, and stakeholder coordination. His role focuses on ensuring that systems, data, and processes run smoothly for schools, clubs, and sporting events.

Alongside his operations work, Katlego is developing his skills in web development and UX design. He enjoys building practical solutions that improve user experiences, simplify processes, and solve real-world challenges.

His long-term goal is to grow within digital sports operations and technology, contributing to platforms that make sport more accessible, organized, and engaging for participants, administrators, and fans alike.
