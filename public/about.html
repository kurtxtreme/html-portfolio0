About Me

I am an Operations Coordinator working at the intersection of sports technology, UX design, and project management. My focus is on building structured, reliable systems that support the delivery of digital sports events. I combine technical understanding with operational execution to ensure platforms run efficiently and users have a clear, functional experience.

In my current role at Its Happening Africa, I manage digital event platforms, live streaming workflows, and system processes for schools and clubs. This includes coordinating stakeholders, maintaining platform accuracy, and improving workflows across fixtures, scoring, and content delivery. I have also worked on projects such as implementing a WhatsApp-based results system to automate data capture and improve turnaround times.

I am particularly interested in how technology can improve access, visibility, and performance in grassroots sport. My goal is to continue developing within web platforms and digital operations, focusing on scalable systems, better user experiences, and more efficient ways to manage and deliver sport through technology.
